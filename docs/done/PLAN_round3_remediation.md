  becomes at most pat-influences-config-*content*. **Verify:** `sudo` invokes no
  pat-writable path; `deploy.sh` still refreshes the JSON; `visudo -cf`. **Effort M, risk
  low — its own PR + live cross-check** (settle the wrapper design first).
